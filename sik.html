ABOUT MYSELF -

About Me -

Teaching Style:
Engaging, interactive, and student-centered. I believe in making maths fun, accessible, and relevant to real-life scenarios. Think games, puzzles, and hands-on activities that foster critical thinking and problem-solving skills!
Specialties:
Algebra, Geometry, Calculus, and Statistics. I'm passionate about helping students overcome maths anxiety and build confidence in their problem-solving abilities.
Personality:
Patient, empathetic, and encouraging. I celebrate mistakes as opportunities for growth and learning. My mantra? *Maths is a journey* not a destination!"
Inspirational Quote:
`Maths is the language with which God has written the universe." - Galileo Galilei

As a maths teacher, I strive to create an engaging and interactive learning environment that makes maths fun, accessible, and relevant to real-life scenarios. My teaching style is student-centered, focusing on building confidence and problem-solving skills through hands-on activities, games, and puzzles. I believe in celebrating mistakes as opportunities for growth and learning, and my approach is patient, empathetic, and encouraging. My goal is to help students overcome maths anxiety and develop a deep understanding of mathematical concepts, from algebra and geometry to calculus and statistics. By exploring the fascinating world of maths together, I aim to inspire a love of learning and a appreciation for the beauty and importance of maths in our lives.
